Steve and Kim Smith

BrickHaven vineyards was founded by Steve Smith in 2013 with his wife Kimberley and son Colin. Steve and Kim share a passion for wine which has lead them to viniculture. Their home in Prior Lake served as ground zero for their grape experiment. This lead them to acquiring 2 additional fields making them the largest family owned vineyard in Minnesota. With grapes acquired from the University of Minnesota, Brickhaven farms has helped set the standard for grape production in an unforgiving climate.

Colin and Amanda Smith

Colin and Amanda married in September 2019 at the Four Oaks field. Soon they plan on moving into their newly built home near the Creekside field, along with their chocolate lab Murphy. Colin manages and operates BrickHaven vineyards.
